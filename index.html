About

Haojian Huang is a master student at The University of Hong Kong. He previously interned at TeleAI, where he was mentored by Professor Xuelong Li and Associate Professor Mulin Chen. His research encompasses Trusted AI, Video Understanding, Embodied AI, and Agentic Systems.

He has published at top-tier venues including AAAI, ICML, NeurIPS, ACM MM, and ACL. He leads CareerSynapse, a student-driven group fostering creativity with large language models. He welcomes any AI research collaboration and is actively seeking Ph.D opportunities.
