About

Haojian Huang is currently an Embodied AI Algorithm Researcher at Knowin, focusing on Embodied AI and Agentic Systems, dedicated to building more interactive and reliable agent-based solutions. He received his M.Sc. in Computer Science from The University of Hong Kong, and has published at top-tier venues such as ICML, NeurIPS, and AAAI.

We are looking for like-minded partners to join our team. If you are passionate about embodied intelligence and believe that robots will eventually enter every household, whether you are an undergraduate or a master's student, feel free to reach out for research collaboration. Outstanding candidates will have the opportunity to be recommended for fully-funded co-supervised Ph.D. programs at HKUST and CUHK. Let's witness robots entering homes together!
