About

Haojian Huang is currently an Embodied AI Algorithm Researcher at Knowin, focusing on Embodied AI and Agentic Systems, dedicated to building more interactive and reliable agent-based solutions. He graduated from The University of Hong Kong, and has published at top-tier venues such as ICML, NeurIPS, and AAAI.

黄浩健，现任诺因智能（Knowin）具身智能算法研究员，研究方向包括具身智能、智能体系统与视频推理，致力于构建更具交互性和可靠性的智能体解决方案。毕业于香港大学，研究成果发表于 ICML、NeurIPS、AAAI 等多个人工智能顶级会议。
